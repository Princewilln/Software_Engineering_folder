Welcome to the World Adventure Quest!
    
    Rules:
    1. Answer the questions correctly to progress through the levels.
    2. You only get a max of three lives. Incorrect answers will decrease your lives.
    3. Gain an extra life by answering two out of three questions correctly in each level.
    
    How to Play:
    1. Read the story passage for each level.
    2. Answer the multiple-choice questions by entering the corresponding number.
    3. Continue until you complete all five levels or run out of lives.
    
    Have fun playing!
